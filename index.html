Who we are

Simba Universal Trading Ltd (Simba Coffee) is a Dar es Salaam-based exporter focused on premium Tanzanian Arabica and robust Robusta lots. Our team works directly at origin across Kilimanjaro, Mbeya, and Songwe, aggregating traceable lots and ensuring transparent payments to farmers.

We specialise in end-to-end export: sourcing, milling oversight, Q-grading, contracts & logistics (FOB/CIF), and trade finance support (LC at sight, escrow, or mobile money for samples). We champion women in coffee, rural employment, and sustainable farming practices.

  • ▸Direct farmer relationships & rural market access
  • ▸Women-led supply chains and youth employment
  • ▸Strict quality control and transparent documentation
  • ▸Flexible incoterms: FOB Dar es Salaam, CIF, or EXW
  • ▸Fast samples & proformas — same-day turnaround
